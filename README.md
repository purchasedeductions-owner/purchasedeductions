# Purchase Deductions

Purchase Deductions is an AI-powered tool that analyzes Amazon purchases and helps businesses identify which expenses may be tax-deductible.

Managing Amazon expenses at scale is messy. Receipts are inconsistent, categories are unclear, and determining deductibility often requires manual review. Purchase Deductions is designed to automate this process and reduce the time and effort required to review business purchases.

## What it does

- Analyzes Amazon purchase data
- Classifies transactions by business expense category
- Flags purchases that may qualify as tax-deductible
- Generates structured reports for review and export

---

## How it works (high level)

Purchase Deductions combines automated rules with AI-based classification to analyze purchase descriptions, metadata, and context. The goal is to provide consistent, reviewable results while keeping humans in control of final decisions.
